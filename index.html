How to Play

  • Match and Blast Candies

    Match three or more candies of the same color to clear them from the board. Create larger matches to earn special candies that can clear rows, columns, or entire sections of the board

  • Complete Level Objectives

    Each level presents unique tasks, such as clearing jelly, breaking barriers, or collecting specific items. Keep an eye on your moves to ensure you achieve the objectives within the limit

  • Utilize Powerful Boosters

    Unlock and use boosters like bombs and rainbow candies to tackle difficult levels. Combine boosters for spectacular effects and maximum impact on the board

  • Renovate the Carnival

    Progress through the game to restore the carnival’s attractions and decor. Each renovation unlocks new chapters in the storyline and additional challenges

  • Participate in Events and Challenges

    Engage in limited-time events to earn exclusive rewards and showcase your skills on the leaderboard. These events add variety and keep the game fresh

FAQ

  • Can I play Manor Carnie Blast offline?
    Yes, you can play most levels offline, which is perfect for gaming on the go. However, features like leaderboards, events, and progress syncing require an internet connection to function properly
  • Is the game suitable for children?
    The game is designed to be family-friendly, featuring colorful visuals and easy-to-understand mechanics. Parents may want to supervise younger players due to the presence of optional in-app purchases
  • Are there in-app purchases?
    Yes, the game offers optional purchases, such as boosters and additional lives. These purchases enhance the experience but are not mandatory to progress or enjoy the game fully
  • How do I get more lives or boosters?
    Lives regenerate over time, or you can purchase them if needed. Boosters are earned through rewards, completing levels, and participating in events. Use them wisely to overcome challenging levels
  • Can I sync my game across devices?
    Absolutely! Connect your game to a Google Play or Facebook account to save your progress. This allows you to continue your adventure seamlessly on different devices
  • What should I do if I encounter an issue?
    If you face any technical issues, use the in-game support feature or contact the developers through the app’s store page. The support team is responsive and dedicated to resolving problems quickly
